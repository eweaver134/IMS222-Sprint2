Meet The Team!

Here at Apple, The Fruit, we only hire highly qualified individuals. We've hired top-notch engineers, designers, and programmers in order to provide our clients with the best experience. Our team is what sets us apart.

Sophia Canino

CEO

Sophia is from Loveland, Ohio, where she first fell in love with the field of robotics. Throughout her high school career, she took many courses on robotics, engineering, and business, going on to work for several robotics companies after graduating. After working in the field for a couple of years, she embarked on a new journey and created this company for robotics consulting.

Kylee Phillips

Robotics Designer

Kylee is from Plain City, Ohio. In high school, she started creating and designing robots, which sparked the beginning of her career. She has years of experience in 3D design, engineering, and robotics design. Her primary area of expertise is giving robots unique personalities.

Leo Nicholson

Chief of Marketing

Leo is from Dayton Ohio and graduated from Northmont High School where he first learned of his passion for marketing. As the Chief of Marketing, Leo is responsible for creating and accelerating the company’s marketing strategy and brand recognition. Previously Leo has worked as the Chief Marketing Officer of similar companies such as Intel and Cvent.

Ethan Weaver

Electrical Engineer

Ethan is from Madison Ohio. It was at Madison High School that Ethan first discovered his passion for engineering. He has since been dedicated to his projects, primarily in robotics. After bouncing between different companies, Ethan found himself in our company. Ethan discovered a new passion for consulting while utilizing his electrical engineering expertise.

Cody Addington

Software Engineer

Cody is from Logan, Ohio. In high school, he had an interest in making small apps and games, helping to push him to make the jump to making bigger and better Software. His main specialties are making innovative new software that helps to push the boundaries of what can be done with robots. He has worked for years on making better software with companies like Minicahsoft, or Sunby.
